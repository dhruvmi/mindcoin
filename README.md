Mindcoin 
================================

What is Mindcoin?

Mindcoin is a clone of Litecoin I made as part of a Youtube series documenting the process. 

Mindcoin properties:

-Initial reward of 20 coins

-Block interval of 5 minutes

-Maximum supply of 350k coins
